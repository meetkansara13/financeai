📄 Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2025

1. Data We Collect
FinanceAI collects transaction data, income records, and budget preferences that you manually enter. We do not collect bank credentials or link to your bank accounts directly.

2. How We Use Your Data
Your financial data is used solely to provide AI-powered insights, spending analysis, and budget recommendations. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage
All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256. Your data is stored on secure servers in India and never leaves the country.

4. AI Processing
Transaction data is processed by our AI models (powered by Groq) to generate insights. This processing is done securely and your data is not used to train AI models.

5. Your Rights
You can export or delete all your data at any time via Settings → Export Data or by contacting support.

6. Contact
For privacy concerns, email: privacy@financeai.in
